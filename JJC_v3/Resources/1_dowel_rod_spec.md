# V3 Dowel Rod Spec

## Purpose

The dowel rod is the rigid center spine for the JJC V3 hollow-body club.

Reference diagram:

```text
Resources/Images/1_dowel_rod_fit.svg
```

## Geometry

```text
Diameter: 12.7 mm
Length: 502 mm
Material: hardwood dowel
```

Printed dowel holes and sockets use a starting clearance diameter of 13.2 mm.

## Balance Role

The dowel is modeled as a 44.5 g hardwood spine with its center at 251 mm from the knob end. It stabilizes the club and keeps the baseline mass close to the middle of the 502 mm length.

## Notes

Measure the actual dowel before finalizing socket clearances.
