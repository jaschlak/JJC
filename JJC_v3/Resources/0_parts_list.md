# JJC V3 Parts List

Goal: create a hollow-body juggling club that keeps the V2 Delphin-style length and handle proportions, but lowers the estimated center of rotation by reducing upper-body mass and using a hollow slip-jointed body.

## Balance Target

```text
Overall length: 502 mm
Target balance point: 255-265 mm from knob end
Target balance ratio: 51-53% of total length
Estimated V3 balance point: 260.6 mm from knob end
Estimated V3 balance ratio: 51.9%
Estimated V3 mass: 266.1 g
```

## Parts

### 1. Dowel Rod

Rigid 1/2 inch hardwood spine.

### 2. Knob Cap

Handle-end bumper and lower counterweight.

### 3. Handle Sleeve

Long tapered grip sleeve.

### 4. Handle-to-Body Collar

Transition from handle diameter to lower body diameter.

### 5. Lower Hollow Body Shell

Closed bottom, open top, and male slip lip that slides into Part 6.

### 6. Upper Hollow Body Shell

Open bottom receiving socket and closed top near the nose.

### 7. Nose Cap

Compact top impact cap.

## Numbering Note

V3 follows the V2 numbering pattern:

```text
Part 5 = lower body shell
Part 6 = upper body shell
Part 7 = nose cap
```

Earlier V3 discussion referred to the two hollow shells as Parts 6 and 7. In this resource set, those same hollow shell ideas are documented as Parts 5 and 6 so V3 remains directly comparable to V2.
