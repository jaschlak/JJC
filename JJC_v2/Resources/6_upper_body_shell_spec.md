# V2 Upper Body Shell Spec

## Purpose

The upper body shell continues from the widest body section and narrows toward the nose cap.

## Geometry

```text
Start outer diameter: 78 mm
Belly outer diameter: 66 mm
End outer diameter: 40 mm
Height: 142 mm
Wall thickness: 1.2 mm
Dowel sleeve OD: 17 mm
Dowel sleeve ID: 13.2 mm
Bulkhead height: 4 mm
```

## Print Orientation

Print standing upright with the 78 mm side on the bed.
