# V2 Knob Cap Spec

## Purpose

The knob cap protects the handle end of the dowel and creates the club's catching stop.

## Geometry

```text
Outer diameter: 33 mm
Height: 28 mm
Dowel socket diameter: 13.2 mm
Socket depth: 22 mm
Closed end thickness: 6 mm
#6 wood screw pilot hole: 2.9 mm through the closed end
Screw outer-clearance chamfer: 10.0 mm outside diameter to 2.9 mm pilot diameter
Screw-side outside shape: lofted asymmetric-style soft end based on 18.0 mm / 3.0 mm fillet target, clamped to preserve screw-seat wall
Socket-side outside shape: lofted asymmetric-style soft end based on 9.0 mm / 10.0 mm fillet target, clamped to preserve socket wall
```

The screw hole is centered on the club axis. The 2.9 mm hole is sized near the root/pilot diameter for a common #6 wood screw, while the 10.0 mm outside chamfer opening creates a visible screw-seat relief at the outside face of the TPU cap.

## Print Orientation

Print closed end down, socket opening up.
