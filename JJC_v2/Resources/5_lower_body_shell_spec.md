# V2 Lower Body Shell Spec

## Purpose

The lower body shell starts at the collar and expands into the widest part of the club body.

## Geometry

```text
Start outer diameter: 42 mm
Belly outer diameter: 70 mm
End outer diameter: 78 mm
Height: 115 mm
Wall thickness: 1.2 mm
Dowel sleeve OD: 17 mm
Dowel sleeve ID: 13.2 mm
Bulkhead height: 4 mm
```

## Print Orientation

Print standing upright with the 42 mm side on the bed unless bed adhesion requires a brim.
